🚘 Ford Edge: Bold Looks, Smart Moves

The Ford Edge is the midsize SUV that blends performance, luxury, and innovation. With turbocharged power and cutting-edge tech, it’s built for drivers who want more edge in every drive.

  • ⚡ Engine: 2.0L EcoBoost® or 2.7L V6 twin-turbo (ST)

  • 🛋️ Interior: Leather-trimmed seats, ambient lighting, panoramic roof
  • 🛡️ Safety: Ford Co-Pilot360™ with evasive steering assist
  • 🎶 Audio: 12-speaker B&O® Sound System
  • 🌍 Drive Modes: Normal, Sport, Slippery, Snow
  • 🧳 Cargo: Hands-free liftgate, fold-flat rear seats
